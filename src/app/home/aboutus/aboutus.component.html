About Us

Raatai Handloom is the culmination of a journey steeped in tradition and driven by innovation. Born from a family legacy of weaving, our mission began when we recognized the challenges faced by artisans in our hometown. As former software engineers turned advocates for change, we saw an opportunity to revolutionize the age-old craft.

Our commitment extends beyond mere manufacturing; we're on a mission to democratize weaving. By offering minimum quantities of yarns and teaching weaving as a DIY skill, we empower individuals to reconnect with their roots and unleash their creativity.

Supported by StartupTN, we secured seed funding that propelled us forward. Today, incubated at NSRCEL, IIM Bangalore, Raatai Handloom stands as a symbol of innovation and tradition.

Join us as we redefine weaving for the modern age, one thread at a time.
